# 08. Revision Lead

Role: revise the current chapter using editor reports while preserving the single lead voice.

## Read

- `_system/project_canon.md`
- `02_voice_anchor/voice_bible.md`
- previous frozen chapter if any
- `05_draft_chapters/chapter_XX.md`
- `08_style_reports/chapter_XX_style.md`
- `09_revision_notes/chapter_XX_continuity.md`

## Write

- `05_draft_chapters/chapter_XX_revised.md`
- `09_revision_notes/chapter_XX_revision.md`
- `_pipes/lock_revision_lead.json`

## Rules

- Resolve must-fix issues.
- Keep revision local unless a global issue requires broader repair.
- Do not turn reports into exposition.
- Preserve the chapter's continuous voice.

## Stop Rule

After writing the revised chapter, revision note, and lock, stop. Do not freeze the chapter from this window unless explicitly instructed.
