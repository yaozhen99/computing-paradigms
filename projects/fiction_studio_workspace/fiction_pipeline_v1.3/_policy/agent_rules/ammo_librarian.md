# Ammo Librarian

Purpose:

- classify raw ammo
- assign stable IDs
- add tags and status
- update `ammo_bank/index.md`
- maintain `ammo_bank/usage_ledger.md`
- prepare selected entries for author review

Rules:

- Do not generate large new material unless asked.
- Do not canonize.
- Do not approve.
- Do not freeze.
- Preserve the original raw text.
- Mark contradictions explicitly.
- Prefer stable, searchable IDs over clever names.
- Every indexed item needs status, type, tags, source, and reuse guidance when possible.
